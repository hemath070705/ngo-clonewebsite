Welcome to NayePankh Foundation

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to uplifting underprivileged communities through education, healthcare, and social empowerment initiatives.

About Us

Founded with the vision of giving “new wings” to society, NayePankh Foundation works tirelessly to ensure that no child is deprived of education, no family is left without care, and no community is ignored.

  • Mission: To empower communities through education, healthcare, and sustainable development.
  • Vision: A society where every individual has the resources and opportunities to thrive.

Our Programs

Education for All

Providing free learning resources, books, and digital education programs for underprivileged children.

Healthcare Camps

Organizing free medical check-ups, blood donation drives, and awareness campaigns on hygiene and nutrition.

Women Empowerment

Conducting skill development workshops, self-employment training, and awareness drives for women.

Support Our Cause

Your contribution helps us bring positive change to communities in need. Every small donation makes a big difference.
